## Part 1: Case Prep
1. Start by removing the drive tray from the case, either discarding or saving for another project.
2. Remove the 2 front panel fans with the screws on either side, and save for later.
3. Remove the standoffs in the bottom plate:
    * Grab a standoff with a pair of vice grips, ensuring a firm grip that won't slip.
    * Bend the standoff back and forth, in all directions until loose.
    * Spin the standoff to ensure it is freed from the case.
    * Tap the standoff out the bottom of the case with a hammer.
    * Flatten the metal back out with a hammer and punch.
    * Repeat for all standoffs.
    * **Alternatively**, the standoffs could be drilled out, leaving a cleaner finish but requiring more tools and work.
4. Touch up the paint around the holes with a black paint pen, if desired.
5. Reinstall the 2 front panel fans.
